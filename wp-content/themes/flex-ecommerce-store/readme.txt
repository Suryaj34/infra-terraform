== Description ==

Flex Ecommerce Store is a powerful, responsive, and fully customizable theme designed for online stores, ecommerce businesses, fashion shops, electronics outlets, furniture stores, grocery shops, supermarkets, digital marketplaces, multi-vendor platforms, clothing boutiques, beauty stores, and retail websites, making it ideal for dropshipping shops, print-on-demand (POD) stores, niche product funnels, and branded online store building. Whether selling physical products, digital downloads, or services, it provides modern ecommerce templates, scalable product catalogs, secure checkout, cart management, product filters, wishlist, product comparison, and multilingual storefronts for a seamless shopping experience across desktops, tablets, and mobile devices. With one-click demo import, pre-designed layouts, fast-loading pages, SEO optimization, and integration with WooCommerce, YITH WooCommerce Wishlist, Contact Form 7, and Yoast SEO, this theme enhances marketing, customer engagement, lead generation, and conversions while allowing effortless customization of layouts, colors, fonts, and branding, making Flex Ecommerce Store a versatile, professional, and conversion-focused solution for startups, POD brands, dropshipping entrepreneurs, and established online retailers seeking scalable and high-performing ecommerce functionality.

== Installation ==

1. In your admin panel, go to Appearance > Themes and click the Add New button.
2. Click Upload Theme and Choose File, then select the theme's .zip file. Click Install Now.
3. Click Activate to use your new theme right away.
